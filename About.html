About US

Welcome to Kitchen's Italian Restaurant, your authentic taste of Italy nestled in the heart of Pittsburgh across five unique locations, McCandless, Gibsonia, South Side, Cranberry and Bethel Park. At Kitchen's, we're passionate about delivering the finest Italian cuisine crafted with love and tradition. Around here, everyone’s familia. Our story begins with a deep-rooted commitment to quality ingredients and time-honored recipes passed down through generations. Our restaurant signifies our pride and commitment to generational impact in our community and beyond.

Begin your lunch or dinner by sharing some of Kitchen’s most popular starters. Everyone loves passing Calamari with fresh Arrabbiata Sauce, Crispy ‘Mozz’ Ravioli, and our Flatbread creations around the table. For your main course, there’s something for every dietary preference including gluten-free and vegetarian. Fans of Parms have the option of Veal, Eggplant or Chicken Parmesan. Light-fare like Grilled Salmon Fresca or hearty thick glazed Pork Chops.
